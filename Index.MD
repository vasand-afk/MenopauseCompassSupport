## Using the Estrogen Balance Sheet

### What Is It?
The **Estrogen Balance Sheet** is a personalized, evidence-based summary that helps you:
- ✅ See potential benefits of estrogen therapy for *your* situation
- ⚠️ Understand risk factors specific to your health history
- 📊 Compare hormone therapy vs. non-hormonal alternatives
- 🗣️ Prepare for informed conversations with your doctor

### How to Access
1. Log at least 7 days of symptoms
2. Complete your health history (family history, uterus status, medical conditions)
3. Tap the **"Balance Sheet"** tab on the dashboard
4. Review your personalized summary

### What Information Does It Use?
- Your symptoms (type, frequency, severity)
- Menstrual status (perimenopausal, menopausal, post-menopausal)
- Uterus status (with/without womb)
- Family history (breast cancer, osteoporosis, cardiovascular disease)
- Personal medical history
- Current medications and supplements

### ⚠️ Important
The Estrogen Balance Sheet is for **informational purposes only**. It does not provide medical advice. Always discuss your results with a qualified healthcare provider before making treatment decisions.

## Symptom Tracking Guide

### Symptoms You Can Track
Menopause Compass supports 40+ symptoms, including:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Vasomotor** | Hot flashes, night sweats, cold flashes |
| **Sleep** | Insomnia, waking up frequently, night waking |
| **Mood** | Mood swings, irritability, anxiety, depression |
| **Cognitive** | Brain fog, memory lapses, difficulty concentrating |
| **Physical** | Joint pain, headaches, weight changes, bloating |
| **Vaginal** | Vaginal dryness, discomfort, urinary urgency |

### How to Log Symptoms
1. Open the app and tap **"Log Symptoms"**
2. Select the symptoms you're experiencing
3. Rate severity on a scale of 1-5
4. Add optional notes (e.g., "Had coffee 1 hour before")
5. Tap **Save**

### Pro Tips
- Log symptoms **at the same time each day** for consistent data
- Log **multiple times per day** for hot flashes—this captures patterns
- Use the **"Notes"** field to record potential triggers (food, stress, sleep)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Menopause Compass free?
The app offers a **7-day free trial**. After that, it's $9.99/month or $79.99/year. You can cancel anytime through your App Store settings.

### Does the app diagnose me?
No. Menopause Compass is for **tracking and informational purposes only**. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional.

### Is my data private?
**Yes.** All your health data stays on your device. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. No data is sent to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it (like exporting a Doctor's Report). For more details, please review our [Privacy Policy](#).

### What happens if I cancel my subscription?
You'll lose access to premium features after your current billing period ends. Your data will not be deleted—it will be saved if you resubscribe.

### Can I use the app if I'm not on HRT?
**Absolutely.** Menopause Compass is designed for everyone—whether you're exploring HRT, using alternatives, or just tracking your symptoms naturally.

### Can I share my data with my doctor?
Yes! The app generates a **Doctor's Report**—a 1-page PDF summary of your symptoms, patterns, and Estrogen Balance Sheet. You can export and share this via email or print it for your appointment.

### What if I have multiple symptoms at once?
Great question! The app allows you to log multiple symptoms in a single entry. Just select all that apply and rate them individually.

### I'm in perimenopause. Can I use this app?
**Yes!** Menopause Compass is built for women in **perimenopause, menopause, and post-menopause**. Tracking from the beginning gives you valuable baseline data.
