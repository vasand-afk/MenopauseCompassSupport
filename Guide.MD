# 🚀 Getting Started with Menopause Compass

Welcome! This guide will help you get the most out of Menopause Compass.

## Step 1: Download and Install
1. Download Menopause Compass from the App Store.
2. Open the app and create your profile.

## Step 2: Complete Your Health Profile
The more accurate your health history, the more personalized your insights will be.
- Navigate to the **Profile** or **Settings** tab.
- Fill in your details, including:
    - Menstrual status (perimenopausal, menopausal, post-menopausal).
    - Uterus status.
    - Family history and personal medical history[citation:3].
    - Current medications and supplements.

## Step 3: Track Your Symptoms Daily
- From the dashboard, tap **"Log Symptoms"** .
- Select the symptoms you're experiencing (e.g., hot flashes, brain fog)[citation:8].
- Rate their severity from 1 (mild) to 5 (severe).
- Add optional notes about potential triggers (e.g., "Had coffee," "Poor sleep").
- Tap **Save**.

## Step 4: Explore Your Insights
After you have been tracking for a few days, the app will start generating insights.
- **Estrogen Balance Sheet:** A personalized summary of the potential pros and cons of estrogen therapy based on your data.
- **AI Correlations:** See what factors (like sleep or caffeine) might be triggering your symptoms.
- **Doctor’s Report:** Generate a summary PDF to share with your healthcare provider.

## Pro Tips
- **Consistency is Key:** Log your symptoms daily, around the same time, for the most reliable data.
- **Use Notes:** The notes field is powerful for identifying patterns. Don't forget to use it!
- **Review Periodically:** Check your insights weekly to see your progress and understand your body's patterns.
