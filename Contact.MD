# 📞 Contact Support

We're here to help! Here’s how you can reach the Menopause Compass team.

## In-App Support (Fastest)
For the quickest response, please use the in-app support feature:
1. Open the Menopause Compass app.
2. Go to **Settings** → **Help & Support**.
3. Tap **Contact Us** and describe your issue.
4. The app will automatically include your device and app version information to help us troubleshoot faster.
